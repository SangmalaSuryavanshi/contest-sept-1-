Trivia

  1. The Beagle is one of the oldest breeds of hounds.
  2. Beagles have a remarkable sense of smell, making them great hunting dogs.
  3. They are known for their friendly demeanor and love for companionship.
  4. Beagles can be vocal; they are known for their distinctive howl.
